Olive - a UX Case Study

Olive was a case study completed for my "UX Immersion Course." It is a health and wellness app that will allow users to securely manage and search for health care providers, track both preventive and treatment appointments, receive reminders for appointments and recommended actions/activities, and search for wellness programs as part of a comprehensive preventive care program.
